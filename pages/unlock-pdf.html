Why unlock a PDF?

Unlocking a PDF is useful when you need easier access to a file you already have legal permission to use. For example, you may want to print, edit, sign, or reorganise a document without entering the password every time.

This tool is intended for legitimate personal and business use only.

Unlock PDF FAQ

Do I need the current password?

Yes. In normal use, you need the existing password to unlock a protected PDF.

Can I unlock a file for someone else?

Only if you are authorised to access and manage that document.

What can I do after unlocking?

After unlocking, you may be able to print, edit, merge, split, or reuse the file more easily.
