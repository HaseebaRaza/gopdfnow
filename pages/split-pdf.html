Why use our Split PDF tool?

Splitting a PDF is useful when you only need a few pages from a larger file. You may want to send selected pages, create smaller attachments, organise records, or separate invoices, reports, and forms into new files.

With GoPDFNow, the process is designed to be clear and simple. Upload your file, choose the pages you need, and create a smaller PDF in a few clicks.

Common reasons to split a PDF

  • Extract important pages from long reports
  • Share only the pages a client or team needs
  • Break large files into smaller email-friendly documents
  • Separate contracts, invoices, or student materials

Split PDF FAQ

How do I split a PDF online?

Upload the file, choose a split option, and process the document. After processing, download the new file or files.

Can I split by page range?

Yes. You can define a range such as 1-3 if your backend route supports custom page extraction.

Will the quality change?

Splitting a PDF normally keeps the original quality because pages are extracted rather than converted.
