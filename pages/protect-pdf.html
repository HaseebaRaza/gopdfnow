PDF Security

Protect PDF with Password

Add password protection to your PDF files to help secure confidential documents, reports, contracts, and records.

Security reminder: Save your password carefully. If forgotten, you may not be able to access the protected file later.

When should you protect a PDF?

Password protection is useful when a PDF contains sensitive or private information. It helps add a basic layer of control before sharing documents by email, cloud storage, or messaging apps.

This can be useful for contracts, HR forms, financial records, client paperwork, student information, and business files.

Protect PDF FAQ

Can I open the PDF without the password later?

No. You will need the password after protection is applied unless you later use an unlock tool.

Is this good for confidential files?

Password protection helps, but your full privacy and data handling policy should also be clear on your website.

Can I share the protected PDF?

Yes, but the recipient will need the password to open it.
