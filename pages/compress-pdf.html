Compressing a PDF means reducing its file size so it becomes easier to send, upload, and store. This is useful for resumes, reports, assignments, invoices, forms, presentations, and scanned documents.

Many people need to make PDF files smaller before sending them by email or uploading them to a portal. A simple online compressor helps avoid delays and makes file handling more convenient.
