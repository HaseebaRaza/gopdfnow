Compress PDF Online

Reduce PDF file size to make documents easier to upload, email, and store.

Tip: Higher compression may reduce file size more, but it can also affect quality depending on your backend settings.

Why compress a PDF?

Large PDF files can be difficult to email, upload to websites, or store in limited spaces. Compressing a PDF helps reduce file size while keeping the document easier to manage.

This is especially useful for scanned files, image-heavy reports, and forms that need to be shared online.

When PDF compression helps most

  • Email attachments with strict size limits
  • Website and portal uploads
  • Saving storage space
  • Faster sharing and downloads

Compress PDF FAQ

Will compression reduce quality?

It can, depending on the compression method and the type of content inside the PDF.

Is this useful for scanned PDFs?

Yes. Scanned PDFs often have large image content, so compression can help a lot.

Can I upload the smaller file afterwards?

Yes. The main purpose is to make your PDF easier to upload and share.
