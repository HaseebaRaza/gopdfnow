Safety & Legal

Report Content or Misuse

Use this page to report copyright concerns, misuse of the service, unlawful activity, or policy violations.

Last updated: 6 April 2026

1. What can be reported

You may contact us to report:

  • Copyright infringement
  • Trade mark infringement
  • Privacy or confidentiality concerns
  • Illegal, harmful, abusive, or fraudulent use of the service
  • Malware, malicious documents, or suspicious uploads
  • Other breaches of our Terms or Acceptable Use rules

2. Information to include in your report

To help us review a report, please include as much detail as possible:

  • Your name and contact details
  • A clear description of the issue
  • The relevant URL, page, file, date, or identifying details
  • Evidence supporting your report
  • A statement explaining your rights or interest in the matter, where relevant

3. Copyright complaints

If you are reporting a copyright issue, please include:

  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • Description of the allegedly infringing material
  • Your contact information
  • A statement that you believe the use is not authorised
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate
  • A statement that you are the rights owner or authorised to act for them

4. Our response

We may investigate reports and may remove content, block access, suspend service, preserve records, or take other action if we believe it is appropriate, necessary, or legally required.

We are not required to take action in response to every report, and we may request further information before acting.

5. False or misleading reports

Do not submit false, malicious, abusive, or misleading reports. We may reject reports, restrict access, or take further action where misuse of this process is suspected.

6. Contact method

You should place your actual email address here once ready, for example:
legal@gopdfnow.com.au or support@gopdfnow.com.au

7. No guarantee of outcome

Submission of a report does not guarantee removal, suspension, or any particular result. We will review matters in good faith based on available information, legal obligations, and service limitations.

Tip: Create a dedicated email such as legal@gopdfnow.com.au or copyright@gopdfnow.com.au and update this page with it.
