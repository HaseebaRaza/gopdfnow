Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GoPDFNow may collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our website and services.

Last updated: 6 April 2026

1. Introduction

GoPDFNow respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit our website, use our tools, contact us, or interact with our services.

By using GoPDFNow, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on how the website operates, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address if you contact us or sign up for updates
  • Uploaded files and file-related metadata needed to process a requested PDF task
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, and usage logs
  • Cookies and analytics data
  • Billing or account data if paid services are introduced later

3. Uploaded files

Uploaded files may be temporarily processed to complete the requested document function, such as merging, compressing, converting, or editing files.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the legal right to upload, process, store, share, and modify any file you submit through our services.

We recommend that you do not upload highly sensitive, confidential, regulated, or legally restricted data unless you are fully satisfied with the security, storage, and operational settings of the service at the time of use.

4. How we may use information

We may use information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website and tools
  • Process uploaded files for requested functions
  • Respond to enquiries and support requests
  • Monitor abuse, fraud, misuse, and security incidents
  • Analyse usage and improve performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to improve website functionality, understand user behaviour, remember preferences, and measure performance.

You may be able to control cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Third-party services

GoPDFNow may rely on third-party providers such as hosting companies, analytics services, payment processors, email tools, security services, and PDF processing APIs.

Those providers may handle data according to their own terms and privacy practices. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services that we do not control.

7. Data storage and retention

Uploaded files, logs, and account-related information may be stored temporarily or longer depending on how the service is configured, legal obligations, backup systems, fraud prevention requirements, or operational needs.

We may delete files and related data at our discretion, but we do not guarantee immediate deletion unless expressly stated by the live service.

8. Security

We may use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures intended to protect information. However, no method of transmission, storage, or processing is completely secure, and we do not guarantee absolute security.

9. Disclosure of information

We may disclose information:

  • To service providers and contractors supporting our operations
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or lawful requests
  • To investigate suspected fraud, abuse, infringement, or security threats
  • As part of a sale, merger, restructure, or transfer of business assets
  • With your consent or at your direction

10. International processing

Your information may be processed or stored in Australia or other countries where our providers operate. By using the service, you acknowledge that data may be transferred across jurisdictions.

11. Your responsibilities

You must not upload files that:

  • Infringe copyright, trade mark, privacy, confidentiality, or other rights
  • Contain illegal, abusive, defamatory, fraudulent, or harmful material
  • Contain malware, malicious code, or unauthorised access tools
  • Violate any law or regulation

12. Children

GoPDFNow is not directed to young children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child in a manner that requires parental consent and such consent has not been obtained, we may delete that information.

13. Access and correction

If you contact us regarding your personal information, we may consider requests for access, correction, or deletion subject to legal, technical, identity verification, and operational limitations.

14. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will apply from the date it is posted on the website.

15. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, complaints, or requests, please contact us through the contact details provided on the website.

Important: This policy is a practical starter policy, not a substitute for formal legal advice tailored to your business model.
